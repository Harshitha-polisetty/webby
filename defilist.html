AI TOOLS

Chatgpt
ChatGPT is a large language model chatbot from OpenAI that uses natural language processing to engage in human-like conversations and generate various types of text. It can answer questions, write different content formats, and even assist with tasks like summarizing information or translating languages.
copilot
Copilot in Word writes, edits, summarizes, and creates alongside you. Business Chat is an entirely new experience that works across all your data and apps. Copilot in Excel works alongside you to help analyze and explore your data. Copilot in Outlook helps you clear your inbox in minutes.
windsurf
Windsurf.ai is an AI-powered code editor, formerly known as Codeium, that aims to revolutionize coding by leveraging AI to enhance developer productivity. It's designed to be a next-generation IDE (Integrated Development Environment) that assists developers with tasks like code completion, bug detection, and even generating code from visual designs.
Deepseek
DeepSeek was founded in December 2023 by Liang Wenfeng, and released its first AI large language model the following year. Not much is known about Mr Liang, who graduated from Zhejiang University with degrees in electronic information engineering and computer science.
cursor ai
Cursor AI is an AI-powered code editor designed to enhance developer productivity by integrating advanced AI features directly into the coding environment. It's a proprietary tool developed by Anysphere Inc.
